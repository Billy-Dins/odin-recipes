Ingredients

  • 2 - 8 oz chicken breasts
  • 1/4c. shredded parmesan
  • 3/4 c. Italian style bread crumbs
  • 1 tbsp. chipotle chilli powder
  • 1 tbsp. freshly ground black peppers
  • 1 tbsp. garlic powder
  • 1/4 c. mayonaise
  • 1/2 c. marinara sauce
  • 1/2 c. mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. preheat Air Fryer to 375 degrees F.
  2. Cut chicken breasts in half, lengthwise and trim.
  3. In medium bowl mix together breadcrumbs, chilli and garlic powder and black pepper.
  4. coat chicken breasts in mayonaise then press into dry ingredients.
  5. place chicken breasts in single layer on bottom rack of air fryer and cook for 6 minutes
  6. Flip the chicken breasts over and topp with marinara sauce and mozzarella cheese. Air Fry for an additioanl 3-4 minutes.
  7. repeat with remaining batch.
