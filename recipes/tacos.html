Carne Asada Tacos

Description

If you've got a FreshCo. near you, they sell this pork shoulder thats pre marinated in carnita rub. This is usually what I use but alternatively you can just cut some chicken into slices and fry with taco seasoning until its crispy... Just get an air fryer tho... peasants.

Ingredients

  • hunk of pork or chicken
  • taco seasoning
  • bunch o' feta
  • some lime juice
  • bit a' cherry tomatos
  • a red onion
  • cilantro
  • Sour cream
  • White corn flour mini tortillas
  • Hot sauce
  • suga
  • olive oil

Steps

  1. preheat Air Fryer to 375 degrees F.
  2. rub hunk of meat with taco seasoning
  3. loosely wrap hunk of meat in tinfoil and cook in air fryer for 30 minutes
  4. lower air fryer heat to 325 degree F. and continue cooking meat for 1 hour
  5. shred meat of choice
  6. dice tomatos and onions, combine with cilantro, sugar and lime juce in bowl. badabing badaboom you've got some peek on a guys hole.
  7. in another bowl combine sour cream, lime juice and cilantro
  8. lightly coat tortillas in olive oil and fry for 30 seconds per side
  9. on tortilla combine meat, pico de guio, sour cream, and hot sauce.
  10. Enjoy
